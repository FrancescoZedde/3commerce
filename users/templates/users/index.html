What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a retail fulfillment method where a store doesn't keep the products it sells in stock. Instead, when a store sells a product, it purchases the item from a third party and has it shipped directly to the customer. One of the major advantages of dropshipping is that it's possible to launch an ecommerce store without having to invest thousands of dollars in inventory up front. Starting a dropshipping business is therefore a low-cost investment, allowing you to start it from anywhere and sell worldwide.

Why SellFast?

As dropshippers we are well aware of the problems faced by those who decide to use this business model. Although it may seem simple there are many factors to consider when deciding to start a dropshipping e-commerce business. The first problem is finding a supplier for your products. And not just any supplier, but one that will guarantee you quality, low prices and proper shipping times. There are also a lot of marketing activities needed to start making your first sales. Not to mention the time it takes to research products, import them into your e-commerce or marketplaces and manage orders. All activities that if done manually take quite a bit of time.

That's why SellFast offers an all-in-one solution for handling all these activities automating each process as much as possible, but still leaving the ability to edit in detail each product.
